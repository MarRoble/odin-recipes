Cajun Pasta in 30 Minutes

Description

A delicious pasta done in half hour less. Check out the ingredients below and how you too can have this for dinner.

  • 4 ounces of linguine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 fresh mushroom
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta enjoy! Just kidding!!

  2. Let it cook for 10 minutes

  3. Get off the phone and prepare the chicken

  4. Place chicken and cajun seasoning in a bowl

  5. In a large skillet over medium heat, mix chicken and butter for about 5 to 7 minutes

  6. Add the peppers, mushroom and cook for another 3 minutes

  7. Reduce heat and add the heavy cream as you stir often

  8. Season with the salt, garlic, and turn off heat

  9. In a larger bowl, toss the pasta with chicken. Sprinkle with the grated Parmesan cheese

  10. Now you can enjoy.
