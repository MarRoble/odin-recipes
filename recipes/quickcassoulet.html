Cassoulet Recipe

Quick Cassoulet

Description

A vegetarian Cassoulet that is good for dinner and outdoors, camping. With a quick preparation, try this recipe and make it your own.

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery diced
  • 15 ounce kidney beans, rinsed
  • 15 ounce of cannellini beans, rinsed
  • 15 ounce of can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • A mushroom or Tofu of our preference for protein

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onion, celery, garlic and cook for a few minutes while stirring in between.
  2. Add Tofu or Mushrooms and cook few more minutes.

    3. Step 2

  3. Pour both Kidney and Cannellini beans. Then add tomatoes, season with bay leaves, thyme, salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and reduce heat to low.
  5. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until vegetables are tender.

    6. Step 3

  6. Remove bay leaves and discard.
  7. Sprinkle parsley over the top and serve.
  8. Enjoy
